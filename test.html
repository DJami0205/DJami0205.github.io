Popular Puppy Breeds

Cane Corso

The Cane Corso is a large and powerful Italian working dog known for its protective instincts. Originally bred for guarding property and hunting big game, they are intelligent and highly trainable. Despite their imposing appearance, Cane Corsos can be loyal and affectionate family dogs with proper socialization. They require strong leadership, early training, and plenty of physical activity.

Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers are friendly, gentle, and eager-to-please dogs that are ideal for families. They were originally bred for retrieving game during hunting, but are now widely loved as therapy and service dogs. Golden Retrievers are easy to train, get along well with people and other pets, and are always up for play. Their cheerful temperament and loyalty make them one of the most popular breeds in the world.
